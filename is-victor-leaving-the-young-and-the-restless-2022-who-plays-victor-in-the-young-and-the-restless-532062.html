The Young And The Restless

The Young and the Restless (also known as Y&R) is a CBS television soap opera produced by William J. Bell and Lee Phillip Bell. The show takes place in a fictitious version of Genoa City, Wisconsin. The Young and the Restless premiered on March 26, 1973, with half-hour episodes airing five times a week. The Young and the Restless initially centered on two families: the affluent Brooks family and the working class Foster family.
