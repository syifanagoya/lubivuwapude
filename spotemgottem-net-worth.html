Early Life

SpotemGottem was born in Jacksonville, Florida, the U.S. on October 19, 2001. He was raised in Jacksonville and he always wanted to make a career in music when he was a young child. He is an expert in freestyle rapping and has also given us several hit songs.

Career

In 2018, SpotemGottem began his music career with his single entitled “Street Gossip”. The song kicked off on his official YouTube channel. He has also rolled out the song on SoundCloud. Just at the age of 17, he released his first-ever song.

The music video gained popularity and his freestyle rapping in the song was loved by people. It’s been two years since its release but his followers still take time to hop onto his song on SoundCloud and YouTube. That song has more than 1.5 million views on YouTube and 1.03 million plays on SoundCloud.

On January 14, 2019, a few days after the debut single, he rolled out his second single entitled “Thug Harmony” and just like his first song, it also began gaining popularity all over.

After that, he kept uploading his songs on YouTube. On January 25, 2019, his new song entitled “My Problem$” was released on YouTube. The official music video of the song kicked off on May 8, 2019, and gathered a good response. The song has more than 1 million views on YouTube.

Since he became familiar with the fact that his songs are being loved by people, he penned a number of more songs and uploaded them on YouTube.

He kicked off a new song named “Why I’m in July 2019. Thereafter, he released several other songs including “Tooka”, “Straight Facts”, “Get Back Mode”, “Adore Me” and “Soulja Mentality”.

SpotemGottem kicked off his new single titled “My legacy” on October 2, 2019, and it has over 2.5 million views on YouTube. On April 21, 2020, his most popular single titled “BeatBox” kicked off and it turned out as the biggest hit of his career with over 19 million views on YouTube.

That song encouraged SpotemGottem to come up with new songs and he received an incredible response from it. He then rolled out a few more singles including “Flaws”, “No Bodyguard”, “Attic”, “Foolery” and “Hotboii”.

He kicked off a new version of Beatbox titled Beatbox 2 on December 18, 2020, which also features another rapper, Pooh Shiesty. The song formed the whole another successful milestone for him.

His recently rolled out singles include “Blick-A-Thon”, “Creep” and “Drill”. On February 4, 2021, he released his last song Drill’s official video.
