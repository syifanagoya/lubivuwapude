Adele set for record number 1 comeback single

Publish date: 2024-05-31

Adele’s epic comeback single ‘Hello’ is set to score the 27 year old Londoner her second number 1 single.

The new track has already racked up over an incredible 165,000 sales in just three days according to the ‘Official Charts Company’ and is averaging 52,000 sales a day.

With these record breaking sales ‘Hello’ is set to become the fastest selling single of 2015.

The fastest selling single of 2015 is currently Ellie Goulding’s massive hit ‘Love Me Like You Do’ which shifted over 173,000 copies in one week, but Adele is on course to smash this record.

‘Hello’ is well on it’s way to securing Adele her second number one single, after ‘Someone Like You’ topped the charts in 2011 for five weeks.

The official chart will be revealed this Friday.
