Early Life and Background

Daniel Franzese was born on May 9, 1978, in New York City.Raised by his parents, Denise, a cafeteria worker, and Ralph R. J. Franzese, a lounge singer, Daniel’s early life in Bensonhurst, Brooklyn, set the stage for his diverse and eclectic career.
