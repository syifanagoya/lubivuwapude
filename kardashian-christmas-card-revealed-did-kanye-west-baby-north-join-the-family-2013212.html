Kardashian Christmas Card Revealed: Did Kanye West, North Join Family?

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Are the Kardashians going Gaga for Christmas? The reality superstar family revealed their annual over-the-top Christmas card on Sunday’s special episode of Keeping Up With the Kardashians: a Very Merry Christmas, and this year’s photo is unlike any other! See other celebrity holiday cards.

Looking like they walked onto the set of a Lady Gaga photo shoot, the Kardashian-Jenner clan’s card features a futuristic, carnival-like set. In the panoramic snapshot, taken by celeb photographer David LaChapelle, the family strikes sexy, bizarre poses in front of gold mannequins and money signs, and on top of piles of magazine covers — including at least one issue of Us Weekly!

This year’s card only includes Kim Kardashian, Khloe Kardashian Odom, Kourtney Kardashian, Kris Jenner, Kylie Jenner, Kendall Jenner, Bruce Jenner, Kourtney Kardashian and her two kids, Mason and Penelope. The entire family is dressed in black, except for little Penelope, 16 months, who is wearing a white dress.

Noticeably missing from the photograph is the majority of the Kardashian family men. Rob Kardashian, Kourtney’s boyfriend Scott Disick, Khloe’s husband Lamar Odom and Kim’s fiance Kanye West and daughter North were not included in the card.

Sunday’s KUWTK Christmas special documented the family’s decision-making process for this year’s photo. LaChappele suggested to the family that only the women, “the matriarchs,” be featured. Bruce disagreed, arguing, “I think that’s a terrible decision. It’s your mom’s and my Christmas card.” Though Kris and Bruce announced their separation in October after 22 years of marriage, Kris ultimately invited him to be on the card.
