WWE Superstar Billy Graham Hospitalized Due To Heart Failure

Publish date: 2024-04-29

WWE legend Billy Graham has been hospitalized currently due to several health complications having heart failure and fluid in his lungs. According to his Facebook post over the weekend, Superstar Billy announced his suffering from heart problems titling the post ‘Atrial Fibrillation’. He wrote,

“ Hi fans, there will be no wrestling post tonight. Just this statement from me. I am currently having heart problems which is the title of this message. I am getting ready to go to the Mayo Clinic to be checked out. I am not feeling well and if the hospital admits me, which I am sure they will for future testing tomorrow, I will have Mike, my FB administrator, give you an update. Superstar B.G.”

His wife, Valerie, then updated about him, saying that he was hooked up to IV Lasix to help get fluid out of his lungs. She added, “he is in afib and heart failure with fluid in his lungs.” Then another, his wife updated, “the fluid is coming off slow but every little bit helps. Still in afib as well. They are doubling his diuretic dose for 3 days to see if that works… can’t do more because it can damage his kidneys.”

Billy’s wife, Valerie, didn’t forget to thank fans for their prayers and support and told them that he would check in with a personal message later by himself.

Superstar Graham had dealt with numerous health issues as he alerted fans in 2019 that he had suffered a heart attack and was in constant heart failure.

Billy Graham Wife And Children Details

WWE legend Billy Graham is currently married to Valerie Belkas. The pair got married in 1981. According to a post on Facebook, it seems like they knew each other in 1978.

The WWE legend’s wife, Valerie, seemed to give an update about her husband whenever something happened to him. In 2020, Valerie gave us an announcement on Facebook regarding her husband being hospitalized due to an infection on his toe.

The pair don't have any children together.

Billy Graham was previously married Madelyn Miluso, with whom he shared two children; one daughter, Capella, born in 1972, and one son named Joey, born in 1975. Then he got divorced from Madelyn in 1978.

In April of 2020, WWE legend Billy had revealed on a Facebook post that his daughter, Capella, and her husband had got infected by COVID-19. He shared that his 47-year-old daughter, Capella, was infected after her husband became positive for Coronavirus.

The Superstar Billy Graham has surely a health issues history. He had cirrhosis when he received a liver transplant in 2002. He had a bowel obstruction from an earlier surgery in 2006. Then Graham had reserved a burial spot at the Green Acres Cemetery in Scottsdale, Arizona, in 2011 when he had announced he had one year left to live without another liver transplant in 2010. Billy was diagnosed with third-stage liver disease and cirrhosis by 2012, and the following year he got hospitalized with double pneumonia and possible heart failure.
