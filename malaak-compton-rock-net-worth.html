Malaak Compton Rock Wiki Biography

Malaak Compton is an Oakland, California-born American actress, businesswoman as well as a social worker who is perhaps better known for being the wife of successful Hollywood actor, Chris Rock. She is a social activist who advocates and works for the betterment of working women and their lifestyles.
